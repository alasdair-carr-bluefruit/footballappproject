Level builds a balanced rotation plan for your whole match — who plays where, when, and how the teams stack up — so every player gets a fair share of game time and every game stays balanced and enjoyable for everyone on the pitch. No spreadsheets, no favourites, no maths on the touchline.

Every coach knows the Sunday-morning maths: who's had a fair go, who's been stuck in goal, which parent is counting minutes from the sideline.

Level does that maths for you — balancing playing time and ability across your squad, so you can focus on the football, and on developing every young player in a fun, friendly, inclusive game.

How it works

A fair plan in seconds

Tell Level your squad and your match. It handles the rest.

Fair by default

Generate a full match rotation that shares game time equally, with every child getting a guaranteed minimum — even in a tight game.

Every position covered

Goalkeeper priorities, defenders, position preferences and formations from 5-a-side to 9-a-side — all respected automatically.

Tinker on the touchline

Not happy with a slot? Drag-and-drop to swap players and Level rebalances the rest. Live match-day controls and a built-in timer.

Season & tournament

Track playing time across a whole season, or balance rotation across back-to-back games on a tournament day. Stats included.

What makes Level different

Fair means balanced — not just equal minutes

Most rotation tools only count time on the pitch. Level goes further: it weighs each player's skill level too, spreading ability evenly across every quarter and every position. So no team is stacked and none is left short — every child is challenged at the right level, and "fair" actually means fair.
