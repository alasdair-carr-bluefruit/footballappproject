Keep things level.

Generate balanced rotation plans so every player gets game time. Track goals, manage minutes across the season, and create match plans in seconds — on the pitch, on your phone.

Start by building your squad!

Add all of your players, include the positions they can play, a rating out of 5 (to help manage team skill balance) and their best position. All of this can be configured at any time throughout the season.
